### Targeting Recommendations

**Interest-Based Targeting:**

- Home decor

**Demographic Targeting:**
- Age: 25-65
- Gender: All
- Income: $50,000+
- Homeowners
- Interest in home improvement

**Behavioral Targeting:**
- Engaged shoppers
- Home and garden enthusiasts
- Technology early adopters
- Online buyers

### Budget Recommendations

**Daily Budget:**
- Awareness: $20-50/day
- Consideration: $30-75/day
- Conversion: $50-100/day
- Retargeting: $25-50/day

**Campaign Duration:**
- Test each ad set for 7-14 days
- Optimize based on performance
- Scale winning ad sets
- Pause underperforming ads

### A/B Testing Ideas

**Test Variables:**
- Headlines (benefit vs. feature)
- Images (before/after vs. lifestyle)
- CTAs (Learn More vs. Get Started)
- Ad copy length (short vs. extended)
- Design style examples

### Performance Metrics

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Conversion rate
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Engagement rate

**Optimization Goals:**
- CTR: > 2%
- CPC: < $1.50
- Conversion: > 3%
- Engagement: > 5%
