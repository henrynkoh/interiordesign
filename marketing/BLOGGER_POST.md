## Tips for Best Results

Based on my extensive testing, here are the tips that will help you get the best results:

### Floor Plan Quality

- **Higher Resolution = Better Results**: Use at least 1200x900 pixels if possible
- **Clear Contrast**: Ensure walls, doors, and windows are clearly visible
- **Clean Images**: Remove unnecessary annotations or clutter
- **Standard Formats**: Use PNG for line drawings, JPG for photographs

### Style Selection

- **Match to Space**: Consider the room's function when selecting a style
- **Consider Architecture**: Match style to your home's architectural style
- **Experiment**: Don't be afraid to try multiple styles
- **Compare Results**: Generate the same floor plan in different styles to see what works best

### Generation Strategy

- **Multiple Generations**: Generate 2-3 times with the same style for more options
- **Style Exploration**: Try all applicable styles to find your favorite
- **Save Everything**: Download all variations you like - you might use them later
- **Take Notes**: Document what works and what doesn't for future reference
