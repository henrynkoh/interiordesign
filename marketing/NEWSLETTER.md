## Design Style Spotlight: Modern

This month, we're featuring the **Modern** design style.

### Characteristics

Modern interior design is characterized by:
- **Clean Lines**: Straight, simple lines without ornamentation
- **Minimalist Aesthetic**: Uncluttered, open spaces
- **Neutral Colors**: Whites, grays, blacks with occasional accent colors
- **Natural Materials**: Wood, stone, metal in simple forms
- **Functional Furniture**: Every piece serves a purpose
- **Abundant Light**: Large windows, open spaces

### Best For

- Contemporary homes and apartments
- Open floor plans
- Minimalist enthusiasts
- Professional workspaces
- Urban living spaces

### Tips for Best Results

- Use clear, simple floor plans
- Emphasize open spaces
- Highlight natural light sources
- Keep furniture minimal
- Focus on clean geometry

### Example Use Case

Sarah, a homeowner in Seattle, used the Modern style for her living room renovation:

"I wanted a clean, uncluttered space that felt open and airy. The Modern style was perfect. I generated multiple variations and showed my contractor exactly what I wanted. The result exceeded my expectations!"

## Pro Tips & Tricks

### Tip 1: Multiple Generations

Don't settle for the first result! Generate 2-3 times with the same style to get more options. Each generation creates unique variations, giving you more choices.

**How to do it:**
1. Generate your first design
2. Download favorites
3. Generate again with same style
4. Compare all variations
5. Select best options

### Tip 2: Style Comparison

Generate the same floor plan in all 6 styles to see which works best. This helps you:
- Understand style differences
- Find your preference
- Show clients options
- Make informed decisions
